COSORO GAMES

Where memories become stories, and stories become games.

COSORO Games is an independent creative brand by Courtney Tan Zhi Xuan, a game developer and digital art hobbyist. A graduate of Game Development and Technology at Nanyang Polytechnic, Courtney will be pursuing Computer Science at the National University of Singapore and is an incoming Technical Artist Intern at Garena.

COSORO Games is driven by a vision to create the Echolyte series—story-driven games centered around memory, identity, and personal growth. Echolyte represents the idea that our memories shape who we are, and can become a powerful force in defining our path.

Each title in the series explores a character burdened by regret, transported into a world where they must confront their past. Through these journeys, players uncover deeply personal stories and emotional truths.

Echolyte: The Curse of Tsuyuhane marks the beginning of this series.
